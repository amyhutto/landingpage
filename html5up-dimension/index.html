About

I’m a dynamic individual with a passion for dancing, singing, and hands-on problem-solving. These interests fuel my creativity and drive, qualities that I bring to my journey into software engineering.

I grew up between Washington D.C. and Augusta, GA, which instilled in me a blend of diverse perspectives and a strong work ethic. After graduating from Augusta Technical College in 2018, I juggled my studies with a role at Staples as a Print and Marketing Associate, where I honed my skills in customer service and attention to detail.

My fascination with technology has only deepened over the years, leading me to explore the world of coding. I am especially drawn to software engineering because it merges my love for solving complex problems with my desire to create innovative solutions. The field's potential to impact lives through technology excites me, and I am eager to contribute to this transformative industry.

I have a particular interest in artificial intelligence (AI) and am enthusiastic about the future possibilities it presents. As I continue to develop my coding skills, I am committed to learning and growing, with the goal of working with leading technology companies such as Amazon, Apple, Google, or Samsung.

In my pursuit of becoming a software engineer, I look forward to embracing challenges, collaborating with like-minded individuals, and making meaningful contributions to the tech world.
